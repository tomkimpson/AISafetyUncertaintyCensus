# Factual-review request template

Subject: Factual review request: public AI safety evaluation reporting study

Hello,

We are preparing a research paper that audits how uncertainty is reported when
public frontier-AI evaluation results are compared with governance thresholds.
The attached dated draft includes results drawn from your organisation's public
documents.

We would be grateful for a factual check of the enclosed rows and highlighted
passages. In particular, please flag:

- an incorrect document version or source locator;
- a copied score, denominator, uncertainty statement, or threshold that does
  not match the public source;
- public uncertainty information that we missed; or
- the intended meaning of any unlabelled `±` quantity.

We are not asking for endorsement or editorial approval. We will correct
verified factual errors and will represent substantive interpretive
disagreements in the paper where relevant. We will treat your reply as
off-the-record unless you explicitly permit quotation or attribution.

Please reply by **[DATE — at least 14 calendar days after sending]**. The frozen
review draft is **[VERSION/HASH]**, and the relevant source rows are attached as
**[FILE]**.

Thank you,

[AUTHORS AND CONTACT]

## Sender checklist

- Freeze draft, repository commit, and derived checksums.
- Attach only the organisation-specific extract plus links to the full record.
- Use a public contact channel or verified organisational address.
- Keep personal addresses and private replies outside the public repository.
- Log sent date, reminder date, deadline, response status, and paper changes.
- Never describe silence as confirmation.
